Water on Mars

We have certainly laid disproved the idea that there are “little green men” living on the surface of Mars. In recent years, thanks to the Spirit and Endeavor rover missions, the depth of our knowledge about the red planet has become much greater.

The climate of Mars was likely very different millions of years ago. Scientists planned the Mars rover mission with one major goal — to test the theory that liquid water once flowed there. The rovers, powered by solar panels that catch rays of light from the sun, have been examining various substances in the soil. Analysis of those substances has given us more and more proof that there truly was water, and perhaps even rain, long ago on Mars.

The two rovers continue to study the geography, geology and atmosphere of the planet. And our understanding of this distant world is rising to new heights.
